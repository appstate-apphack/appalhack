What is AppHack?

AppHack is a 24-hour student-run hackathon hosted at Appalachian State University. At AppHack, teams of students get involved in the software development process including design, development, and marketing disciplines. In addition to being a highly productive environment, AppHack is loads of fun! We offer awesome prizes for outstanding projects!

AppHack 2024 will start on April 1st and go through the 2nd. Our main form of communications will be through Discord. To get an invite to our Discord, please register above!
